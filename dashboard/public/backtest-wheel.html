BTC Options Wheel — Backtest with Deribit DVOL
Delta-Based Adaptive Wheel Strategy (Δ ≈ 0.20):
1 Calculate VRP = IV (Deribit DVOL) - RV (30-day realized vol)
2 VRP > 20: Sell Δ base×0.50 put (far OTM, IV rich → delta halved)
3 VRP 10~20: Sell Δ base×0.75 put (moderate OTM → delta reduced)
4 VRP 0~10: Sell Δ base put (use base delta as-is)
5 VRP < 0: Pause selling — IV underpriced, not worth the risk
6 If assigned (bought BTC): sell covered call at Δ0.20 call, repeat until called away
? Why Delta? — BS Delta auto-adjusts strike for volatility: high IV → strike further OTM; low IV → closer ATM. Same risk profile regardless of market regime.
Risk Controls:
A Put Spread — buy protective put below sell strike. Max loss per trade = spread width. Crash → hedge pays, stay in cash.
B Stop-Loss — holding BTC after assignment? If price drops X% below entry, sell immediately. Cut losses, go back to selling puts.
